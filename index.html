01 — About

The work comes first.
It always has.

Nicolás Devoto is a young actor drawn to honest, grounded storytelling. On set he is known for two things: listening like the camera isn’t there, and showing up prepared like the role depends on it — because it does.

His recent work includes the short film “Home,” where he carried scenes opposite older cast with a quiet, natural presence that directors don’t have to manufacture. He takes direction fast, resets without ego, and treats every table read, rehearsal, and take as a chance to get better.

He’s early in his career — and that’s the point. Nicolás is building a body of work one truthful performance at a time, and he’s looking for the roles that will stretch him next.
